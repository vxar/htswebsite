Hamilton Tamil Sangam is a non-profitable organization formed in 2020 with an objective of uniting the Tamil community in and around Hamilton, Ontario - Canada. Our efforts are driven to build bonding among Tamil community and integrate cultural heritage among Tamil speaking families. Founded 5 years ago, HTS has grown from a few families to over 100+ families, organizing events every year to gather Tamil-speaking people.

About Us

Hamilton Tamil Sangam (HTS) was established in 2020 to foster unity and cultural integration among Tamil-speaking families in Hamilton, Ontario, Canada. Over the past 5 years, we've expanded from a small group of families to a vibrant community of over 100 families. We organize annual events that celebrate our rich Tamil heritage, promote cultural exchange, and strengthen community bonds.

Our mission is to create a supportive environment where Tamil culture thrives, and families can connect, share, and grow together in Canada.
